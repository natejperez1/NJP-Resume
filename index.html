About Me


 I am a Computer Systems Engineer and have worked in the industry since I was 16. Ever since my first job as a computer repair technician I was determined to learn everything I could about any and all IT related facets. As the years went on and my experience grew I became more and more fascinated with server administration and networking. Like most computer nerds, I live and breath IT; I have way more computers than any healthy human being should and I built a Highly Available Hyper-V Cluster to help facilitate a better understanding of the latest and greatest products. Throughout the last two years or so, I have obtained numerous certifications including A+, MCTS: Configuring Windows 7 and MCSA: Windows 7/MCITP: Enterprise Desktop Administrator. I am currently studying for the CCNA exams and Network+ and I plan on continuing to earn 3 more Microsoft certifications in order to obtain an MCSE. Nothing worth learning comes easy and I live by that notion, constantly working towards improving myself both professionally and personally has not been easy but the results of my persistence has left me yearning for more.
